About Us Page

We are a team of five students at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte, who came up with an idea and decided to create a website. We worked together to create a product that we felt was missing and could be beneficial to a lot of people. This website is a way to help users, mainly college students keep track of their goals and provide steps to help them accomplish their goals.


Use This Website To...

  • Help you track your progress for all your goals
  • Provide you with resources that are specific to your needs
  • Help you network and get your questions answered by recent grads and professionals
